About Ward Chemical

Ward Chemical has been producing premium quality RC model airplane fuel for over 35 years. Our commitment to quality, consistency, and performance has made us a trusted name in the RC community.

All our fuels are mixed in small batches to ensure quality control and are tested extensively before shipping. We use only the highest quality ingredients, including top-grade methanol, synthetic oils, and proprietary additives.

Founded by RC enthusiast Robert Ward in 1985, our company has grown from a small garage operation to one of the leading suppliers of model engine fuel in North America. Despite our growth, we maintain the same attention to detail and quality that built our reputation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the shelf life of your fuel?

When stored properly in a sealed container away from direct sunlight, our fuel has a shelf life of approximately 2 years. We recommend using within 1 year of purchase for optimal performance.

How should I store my fuel?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep containers tightly sealed when not in use to prevent moisture absorption and evaporation of methanol.

Which fuel is best for my engine?

This depends on your engine type and application. Generally, sport flyers do well with our 15% mix, while competitive flying benefits from our 30% mix. Helicopter engines perform best with our specialized Heli-Mix. Always consult your engine manufacturer's recommendations.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select countries, subject to hazardous material shipping regulations. Please contact us for specific information about international shipping to your location.

Safety Information

RC model fuel contains methanol and nitromethane, which require proper handling. Please observe these safety guidelines:

  • Keep away from heat, sparks, and open flames
  • Store in original container in a cool, well-ventilated place
  • Keep out of reach of children and pets
  • Avoid contact with skin and eyes
  • Use in well-ventilated areas only
  • Do not ingest
  • Wash hands thoroughly after handling
